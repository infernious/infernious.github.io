my philosophy

I really don't like the way chemistry textbooks are written. They are

  • expensive,
  • unstylistic,
  • convoluted,
  • unstructured,
  • and incomplete.

Chemistry is hard. Why? Because learning materials lack coherence. What does this result in? Something that's hard to learn. This isn't made any better by the circular nature of chemistry. I am making this website to amend this.

How does my website fix this?

  • This will be an ad-free website.
  • Hopefully you can also see my style here.
  • My style is (hopefully) conversational and easy-to-follow.

Moreover, I plan to write content in a so-called "Heming-way," filled with everything you need to know to understand chemistry (including areas where chemists disagree). I don't wish to present chemistry as just a collection of unrelated facts. I want to portray it as an argument where everything makes logical sense based on a few fundamental pillars.

In any case, welcome to this work-in-progress project.
